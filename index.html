ABOUT DEPARTMENT

The KKR & KSR Institute of Technology and Sciences was established COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING stream in the year of 2008 offers B.Tech in CSE with intake 180 every Academic year and M.Tech in CSE with intake 18 every Academic year. The Department consists of 6 Computer Labs having 368 systems with high configuration and fully Air Conditioned, along with 1 R&D Centre. Almost all fields are computerized to have an ease of handling the problems of designing, manufacturing, maintenance, servicing, researching, marketing and accounting. The B.Tech computer engineering program includes computer operations on different languages, data generation, collection and utilization of information. The Department is implementing OBE based education. The Department has good MOU's with ETI Labs Pvt. Ltd, Kashmere Gate Delhi, Microsoft, NextgenC, AICTE Margadarshan, IDEA Labs, Red Hat Linux, Orange Research Labs, APSSDC.... The Department of CSE has well-qualified and experienced faculty with 6 Doctorates and will look of Software & Hardware facilites and requirements of entire college. Our Graduates have been consistently places in Various compaines like Capgemini, Congnizant, Infosys, Wipro, Virtusa Polaris, Mind Tree, Infomerica, Genpact, Glenwood Systems, Syntel.
